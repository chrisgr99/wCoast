**Adjust a scope:** you can also watch the plucks from the low pass gate patch you built earlier, and resize the scope to see them well.

> **Do this** — Put a scope on the "Quad Low Pass Gate" channel A output (OUT) — your earlier pluck patch, with its clock running — and watch the pulses go by. Drag the scope's right edge to widen it, fitting more pulses on screen at once. Then turn channel A's Decay up and watch each pulse stretch out longer, or change the oscillator feeding the gate and see how the pulse's shape changes.
